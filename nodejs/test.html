About Cakepop

Cakepop is a Chrome extension that allows you to quickly search up information without opening a new tab. Based off of an action, be it a quick snapshot or highlighting of text, the chrome extension will pull up relevant information and links in a side preview which can be accessed from the corner of the page. Cakepop displays info in clean, material fashion and sorts out info by four categories: People, Places, Events, and Web. Under People, you can find details about people mentioned in the page, such as age and recent news. Under Places, you can find info of places like ratings and location. Under Events, you can find info about events mentioned within the page. Under Web, you can find links and relevant websites.

It's small and sweet
